# Articles

### Algorithms

- [Electricity Standard Load Profiles (Standardlastprofile
  Strom)](https://flrd.github.io/standardlastprofile/articles/slp-electricity.md):

  BDEW Standardlastprofile Strom algorithm in R: period mapping,
  dynamization function, and public holiday handling for profiles H0,
  G0-G6, L0-L2, H25, G25, L25, P25, S25.

- [Gas Standard Load Profiles (Standardlastprofile
  Gas)](https://flrd.github.io/standardlastprofile/articles/slp-gas.md):

  BDEW Standardlastprofile Gas in R: the SigLinDe synthetic procedure,
  Kundenwert, and weekday factors for 15 gas profiles (HEF, HMF, HKO,
  GKO, and 11 more).

### Gas profile function and parameters

- [SigLinDe Parameters and Weekday Factors for Gas
  SLP](https://flrd.github.io/standardlastprofile/articles/slp-gas-parameters.md):

  SigLinDe profile function coefficients (A, B, C, D, mH, bH, mW, bW)
  and weekday factors for all 15 BDEW Standardlastprofile Gas - variants
  33 and 34, from the BDEW Leitfaden (as of 2026-03-27).
